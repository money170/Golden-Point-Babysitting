Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance to ensure availability, especially for weekend services.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

We understand that plans can change. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance for cancellations or rescheduling.

Do you provide care for children with special needs?

Yes, our babysitters are trained to work with children of all abilities. Please let us know about any specific needs when booking.

What should I prepare for the babysitter?

We'll provide a detailed checklist, but generally include emergency contacts, medical information, and any specific routines or preferences.

How do you handle emergencies?

All our babysitters are Red Cross certified and trained in emergency response. They'll contact you immediately and follow established protocols.

Can I meet the caregiver before booking?

Absolutely! We encourage meet-and-greets to ensure everyone is comfortable. Contact us to arrange a meeting.
