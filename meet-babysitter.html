Our Qualifications

Red Cross Certification

All 3 babysitters are Red Cross certified, ensuring they have the necessary training in first aid, CPR, and emergency response procedures.

4-Hour Training Course

All babysitters have completed an intensive 4-hour training course covering child safety, development, and best practices in childcare.

Big Buddy Experience

Our team has valuable experience as "Big Buddies" for kindergartners, providing mentorship and care to young children in educational settings.

Volunteer Background

All caregivers have extensive volunteer experience, demonstrating their commitment to helping others and serving their community.

Genuine Love for Children
