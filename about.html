About Us

Dedicated to providing exceptional weekend babysitting for families in Ann Arbor

Our Mission

At Golden Point Babysitting, we believe every family deserves peace of mind when they need weekend babysitting for their children. Our mission is to provide professional, reliable, and caring weekend babysitting services that allow parents to enjoy their time away knowing their children are in safe, capable hands.

We understand that finding trustworthy weekend babysitting can be challenging, which is why we've built our service around the principles of safety, reliability, and genuine care for children. Our team of 3 Red Cross certified babysitters brings both professional training and personal dedication to every assignment.

Our Values

Safety First

Every caregiver is Red Cross certified and trained in emergency response. Your child's safety is our top priority.

Trust & Reliability

We build lasting relationships with families through consistent, dependable service and open communication.

Genuine Care

Our caregivers genuinely love working with children and bring enthusiasm and patience to every interaction.

Comfort & Familiarity

We provide care in your child's own home, maintaining their routine and creating a comfortable environment.
