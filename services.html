Our Services

Professional weekend babysitting for families in Ann Arbor, Michigan

Weekend Babysitting

Our specialized weekend babysitting service provides parents with peace of mind during weekend days and evenings. Our team of 3 Red Cross certified babysitters are experienced in handling care for children of all ages, ensuring a safe and comfortable environment for your little ones.

What's Included:

  • Professional supervision
  • Activity planning and engagement
  • Emergency response capability
  • Light housekeeping related to children
  • Safety monitoring and care
  • Flexible weekend scheduling

Weekend Availability

Available Friday and Saturday to accommodate your weekend plans. Perfect for date nights, events, errands, or much-needed rest.

In-Home Service

We come to your home, providing care in your child's familiar environment. This reduces stress and maintains their normal routine.

3 Experienced Babysitters

Our team of 3 Red Cross certified babysitters provides reliable and consistent overnight care for your family.

Age Range

We care for children 12 months and older. Our caregivers are trained to handle the specific needs of different age groups.
